**Sales Analysis Power BI Dashboard**

This project features a dynamic Power BI report analyzing global sales distribution with a deep dive into the United States market performance.

**Key Features**

Dynamic Metrics: Toggle between absolute Total Sales and % Share across territories.

US Market Deep-Dive: Detailed analysis of the $9M US market, covering demographics and product trends.

Demographic Insights: Sales breakdown by customer income, gender, and commute distance.

Product Analytics: Performance tracking by product color and standard cost.

**Visual Highlights**

Top Market: United States accounts for 32% of total global sales.

Top Products: Red and Black items drive the highest revenue.

**Usage**

Open the .pbix file in Power BI Desktop.

Use the main slicers to filter by country and use the Measure Switcher to toggle the view between percentages and absolute totals.

Explore the US Detailed tab for granular regional trends.
